The Haitang Bay Gloria Sanya is located along beautiful Haitang Bayís National Coast and is a unique treasure on Hainan Island, which is also known as the Hawaii of the Orient. Conveniently located just 30 minutes from Sanya Phoenix International Airport, Sanya city center and other touristic attractions, the Haitang Bay Gloria Sanya faces the Wuzhizhou Island Diving Area, famous for its abundant marine wildlife. The private white sandy beach and crystal clear waters off the Haitang Bay coast provide ample opportunities for waters ports and other recreational activities. The Haitang Bay Gloria Sanya offers deluxe services and facilities in its aim to become the ultimate escape for luxury travelers. The resort boasts 449 elegantly appointed guestrooms and suites, including 374 oversized guest rooms, 75 suites and studios with distinctive amenities and a spectacular presidential suite. All rooms and suites are equipped with an individual balcony overlooking the breathtaking South China Sea or natural greenery. A selection of nine restaurants, bars and lounges provides a diversified and trendy culinary experience and offers an exciting blend of Asian and Western cuisines.

The resortís experienced meetings and events team ensures all meeting and events are well-planned, well-organized and memorable. The huge pillar-less grand ballroom can be flexibly divided and, together with the 10 multi-function rooms and a business center provides a perfect venue for high level business meetings, elaborately themed parties and intimate events. The Haitang Bay Gloria Sanya also has 4,000-square meters large private lawns, which are the perfect venues for special social occasions, celebrations or team building activities. The resortís private sandy beach, the wide selection of recreational outdoor activities, a total of 5,000-square meters of swimming pools, natural hot spring pools and children pools, two floodlit tennis courts, a PGA golf driving range, a Kidís Club, Health Club and a world class SPA center undoubtedly make the Haitang Bay Gloria Sanya ìa good place to be!î.
